SwiftUI Previews are a great way to 调试 your SwiftUI views without running your entire application. To use SwiftUI Previews, simply select the view you want to preview and click the "Preview" button in Xcode's toolbar. SwiftUI Previews allow you to see how your views will look at different sizes and orientations, and you can even interact with them to see how they respond to user input.
